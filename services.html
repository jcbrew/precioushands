Companionship

Help individuals and families with their everyday tasks and responsibilities by performing the following duties in their personal homes. Companionship can also include spending quality time with a family member e.g. conversation, sharing a movie, family histories, day outings ECT.

  • Light cooking
  • Light cleaning
  • Taking out daily garbage
  • Medication distribution upon request, advised by a physician or family member
  • Organization of household
  • Shopping services and errands
  • Transportation anywhere
  • Laundry services
  • Travel companions

Hospice Care advised by a physician or family member

  • Grooming/ personal hygiene
  • Diaper changes
  • Catheter changes and monitoring
  • Bathing with consent from Power of Attorney
  • Insulin shots
  • Hand and tube feeding
  • Blood Pressure monitoring
  • And many more Hospice services offered
  • Other special requests honored

Our goal is to keep our clients comfortable, healthy, and happily in their homes forever. We know that as individuals age they prefer to be in the comfort of their own homes rather than in a nursing/assisted living facility. Precious Hands duty is committed to helping seniors gradually, “age in peace” and honors this wish to our fullest potential.
